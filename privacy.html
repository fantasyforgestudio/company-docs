This Privacy Policy applies worldwide to FantasyForge Studio, LLC’s websites, games, mobile applications, and related services (collectively, the “Services”). If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, you should not use the Services.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT & SENSITIVE DATA

  • Voluntary Information: We collect your name and email if you contact us for support or report royalties at info@fantasyforgestudio.online.
  • Account & Tier Data: For our mobile applications, we collect credentials (e.g., .edu email verification) to provide educational, fictional, or adult content tiers.
  • Camera Data: Our app requires camera access to identify clouds. We do not upload, store, or share images containing identifiable people. Processing occurs locally on-device or via secure identification algorithms.
  • Age Verification: To access adult-oriented content, we use third-party age-verification methods to comply with applicable laws, including the Virginia Age-Verification Requirements for Harms to Minors (Va. Code § 18.2-391.2 et seq.). We do not use age-verification data for marketing or any purpose other than legal compliance.
  • Usage Data & Identifiers: We (and our analytics and advertising partners) collect IP addresses, device identifiers (IDFA/AAID), browser type, and approximate location. We use Google Analytics and Firebase to operate the Services, measure performance, and deliver advertising.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use your data to:

  • Deliver specific content (Educational vs. Adult) based on your verified user role.
  • Process voluntary donations and verify royalty reporting.
  • Refine accessibility checking algorithms.
  • Personalized Advertising: We use Google AdMob and Firebase to show ads relevant to your interests. You may be able to subscribe to an ad-free option where offered.

3. THIRD-PARTY SHARING & CONSOLES

  • No Sale of Data: We do not sell your personal information for money.
  • Service Providers: We share data with vendors who help us operate (e.g., Stripe for payments, AWS for hosting, Google/Firebase for analytics and ads).
  • Stripe & Payment Security: All payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store or have access to your full credit card or bank account information.
  • Google Data Practices: For more on how Google processes data, please visit: google.com
  • Console Platforms: If you access our games via PlayStation, Xbox, or Switch, those platforms may share “Online IDs” with us. You are subject to their respective privacy policies.

4. DATA SECURITY, RETENTION & TRANSFERS

  • Security: We use industry-standard encryption; however, no method of transmission is 100% secure.
  • Data Destruction: We practice data minimization. Personally identifiable information (PII) is destroyed or de-identified when no longer necessary, unless legal, regulatory, or tax obligations (such as royalty audit windows) require longer retention.
  • International Transfers: We are located in the United States. By using our Services, you acknowledge that your information will be transferred to and processed in the U.S. For users in the EU or UK, we rely on standard contractual clauses to protect your data.

5. COOKIES & TRACKING

We use cookies and similar technologies to recognize you. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, but some parts of our Services may not function properly. We currently do not respond to "Do Not Track" (DNT) signals.

6. CHILDREN’S PRIVACY (COPPA)

Our Services are not directed to children under 13. If we discover we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

7. YOUR RIGHTS & AD OPT-OUTS

Depending on your location (e.g., Virginia VCDPA, California CCPA, or EU GDPR), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your data, and to opt out of targeted advertising.

  • To Exercise Rights: Contact info@fantasyforgestudio.online.
  • Ad Opt-Out: You can reset or limit your advertising ID in your mobile device settings (e.g., "Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS or "Opt out of Interest-Based Ads" on Android).

8. BUSINESS TRANSFERS & UPDATES

  • Business Transfers: If FantasyForge Studio, LLC is involved in a merger or sale, your info may be transferred as a business asset. We will notify you of any change in ownership.
  • Changes to Policy: We may update this policy at any time. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates the most recent changes.
